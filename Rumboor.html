Welcome to Rumboor

About Rumboor

Rumboor, one of the valleys in the Kalash region of Chitral, Pakistan, is a place of remarkable beauty and cultural significance. Nestled in the Hindu Kush mountain range, it is known for its lush greenery, terraced fields, and crystal-clear streams. The valley is part of the unique Kalash community, renowned for their vibrant traditions, distinct language, and colorful attire.

Cultural Highlights

The culture of Rumboor, a valley in the Kalash region of Chitral, Pakistan, is deeply rooted in the traditions of the Kalash people, one of the smallest and most unique indigenous communities in the world. The Kalash culture is renowned for its vibrant festivals, ancient rituals, and a distinct way of life that has been preserved for centuries.
