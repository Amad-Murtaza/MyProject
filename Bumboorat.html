Discover Chitral

About the Place

Chitral, a valley in northern Pakistan, is known for its breathtaking scenery, diverse culture, and the towering Hindu Kush mountain range. It's a destination for nature lovers, adventurers, and history enthusiasts.

Hotels in Chitral

Hotel Chitral Inn

Located in the heart of Chitral, offering modern amenities with traditional charm.

Hindu Kush Heights

A luxury resort with panoramic views of the mountains.

PTDC Motel

Government-operated motel ideal for budget travelers.

Road Conditions News

Lowari Tunnel Update

The Lowari Tunnel is open for traffic, easing access to Chitral despite heavy snowfall.

Shandur Pass Alert

Travelers are advised to check weather forecasts before heading to Shandur Pass due to icy roads.
